Binglun Wang

I'm a PhD student at University College London (UCL) VECG group, advised by Dr. He Wang and Prof. Niloy Mitra. Before my PhD study, I worked as Research Intern at Adobe Research London and UCL with with Dr. Duygu Ceylan, Prof. Niloy Mitra and Kinross Prof. Ray Dolan.

My research interest span Computer Graphics, Machine Learning and Computational Physics, all are important to me in exploring inverse problem of physically digital creation. The ultimate goal is to control real world physics. Currently, I focus on the research of physics-based character-fluid simulation.

I organise monthly AI & Science seminars/discussions in non-profit organization Goodenough College London. You can see our events here.
